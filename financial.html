QuickBooks

If you run a small to medium-sized business and want to ensure you can track all financial information and create reports summarizing any aspect of your business

 

Accounting Setup or Transfer (Fractional Controller)

Leaving critical accounting department functions in the hands of unqualified, in-house personnel as you grow can lead to tax oversights, compliance issues, and poor financial planning. As your business grows in size or complexity, a fractional controller can help you manage and leverage your financial information.

 

QuickStart QuickBooks Setup and Support

We’ll show you how to get started with QuickBooks which will enable you to Customize your invoices, Enable and receive payments, Set up sales, tax etc

 

Accounting Services (bookkeeping + analysis)

Keeping clear business records is important. It helps you understand how much money is owed to you, how much you owe and will prevent you from making costly – not to mention illegal – errors. It can also help you monitor your business and identify sources of income.

 

Fractional Controller (monitoring 3rd party bookkeeping)

A Controller is responsible for the finance operations of a company. This includes financial reporting and maintenance of an adequate system of bookkeeping records. Not to mention a comprehensive set of controls and budgets designed to mitigate risk.

 

Financial Statements Analysis, Comparisons

we will help look at a number of ratios on a company’s income statement to determine how efficiently it generates profits and shareholder value.

 

Cash Flow Management and Analysis

Without proper cash management, regardless of how fast a firm’s sales or reported profits on the income statement are growing, a firm cannot survive without carefully ensuring that it takes in more cash than it sends out the door.

 

KPI Analysis, Benchmarking, Hot Sheets, Trend Analysis

By understanding these metrics, you can be better positioned to know how the business is performing from a financial perspective. You can then use this knowledge to adjust the goals of your department or team and contribute to critical strategic objectives.

 

Budgeting, Forecasting, Goal Setting

We will help you identifying, plans and predict the prospective financial data that reflects expectations of revenue and profit.
